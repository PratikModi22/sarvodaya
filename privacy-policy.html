Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE: APRIL 25, 2025  |  SARVODAYA AUTOMATION · PUNE, MAHARASHTRA, INDIA

01 / Introduction

Sarvodaya Automation ("we", "our", "us") is an automation consultancy registered in Pune, Maharashtra, India. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you visit our website (sarvodayaautomation.com) or use our services — including integrations with the WhatsApp Business API and Meta platforms.

By using our website or engaging our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

02 / Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and business name — collected via contact forms or during onboarding
  • WhatsApp number and message content when you interact with us or our clients via WhatsApp Business
  • Company information and project details provided during our engagement

Technical & Usage Data:

  • IP address, browser type, device type and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs
  • Cookie identifiers and session data

Meta / Facebook Platform Data:

  • When accessing services via Facebook Login or WhatsApp Business API, we may receive your profile name, account identifiers, and profile picture as permitted by Meta's Platform Terms
  • WhatsApp message content and metadata when operating automation workflows on behalf of our clients

03 / How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver our automation services
  • To communicate with you via email or WhatsApp regarding your project or service
  • To send operational updates, alerts, and support messages
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations under Indian and international law
  • To detect and prevent fraud, misuse, or security incidents

04 / WhatsApp Business API & Meta Data Handling

⚠️ Important: Sarvodaya Automation uses the WhatsApp Business API solely to provide the automation services explicitly requested by our clients and their end-users. We do not use WhatsApp message data for advertising, profiling, re-marketing, or any purpose beyond delivering the agreed service.

  • WhatsApp messages are processed only as operationally necessary to run your workflow
  • Message content is not stored beyond what is required (maximum 90 days unless legally required)
  • We do not sell, share, license, or monetise WhatsApp user data to any third party
  • Users may opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time by replying STOP
  • All WhatsApp integrations comply with Meta's Platform Terms and WhatsApp Business Policy
  • API credentials and access tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide the service

Our use of Meta Platform data strictly follows Meta's Platform Terms and is limited to providing the service the user has explicitly requested. We do not use Meta data for any purpose inconsistent with those terms.

05 / Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent — for marketing communications and optional data collection
  • Contract — to fulfil the automation services you have engaged us to provide
  • Legitimate Interests — for analytics, security, fraud prevention, and service improvement
  • Legal Obligation — to comply with applicable laws in India and internationally

06 / Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted operational partners solely for the purpose of delivering our services:

  • Meta / WhatsApp — for WhatsApp Business API messaging (WhatsApp Privacy Policy)
  • n8n GmbH — workflow automation infrastructure (n8n Privacy Policy)
  • Make / Celonis SE — automation platform (Make Privacy Notice)
  • Cloud hosting providers — AWS, DigitalOcean, or equivalent for secure infrastructure
  • Analytics tools — anonymised website traffic analytics only

All third-party providers are contractually required to handle data securely and only for the stated operational purpose.

07 / Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — anonymised data to understand site traffic patterns
  • Functional cookies — to remember your preferences (e.g. cookie consent)

You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling analytics cookies does not affect core site functionality.

08 / Data Retention

  • Client project data: retained for up to 3 years after project completion
  • Contact form submissions: retained for 12 months
  • WhatsApp message logs: maximum 90 days unless a longer period is legally required
  • Cookie consent records: 12 months

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

09 / Your Rights

Under GDPR and India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent — withdraw previously given consent at any time
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@sarvodayaautomation.com

10 / Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that data has been collected from a person under 18, we will delete it immediately. Contact us if you believe we have collected data from a minor.

11 / International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside India (for example, in the EU or USA via our service providers). In such cases, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent mechanisms as required by applicable law.

12 / Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure credential management for all API keys and access tokens

13 / Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy here with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
