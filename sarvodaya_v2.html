Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE: APRIL 25, 2025  |  SARVODAYA AUTOMATION · PUNE, INDIA

01 / Introduction

Sarvodaya Automation ("we", "our", "us") is an automation consultancy based in Pune, Maharashtra, India. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website or services — including integrations with the WhatsApp Business API and Meta platforms.

02 / Information We Collect

Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, WhatsApp number, business name — collected via contact forms or during onboarding.

Usage Data: IP address, browser/device info, pages visited, session duration, cookie data.

Meta / WhatsApp Data: When you interact via WhatsApp Business or Facebook Login, we may receive your profile name, account identifiers, and message content as permitted by Meta's Platform Terms.

03 / How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver our automation services and respond to inquiries
  • To communicate via email or WhatsApp about your project
  • To send service updates and operational messages
  • To improve our website and service quality
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian and international law

04 / WhatsApp & Meta Data Handling

⚠️ Critical: We use WhatsApp Business API solely to deliver the automation service you have requested. We do not use WhatsApp message data for advertising, profiling, or any secondary purpose.

  • Messages are processed only as necessary to run your workflow
  • Message content is not stored beyond operational necessity (max 90 days)
  • We do not sell, share, or license WhatsApp data to any third party for commercial use
  • Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP via WhatsApp
  • All integrations comply with Meta's Platform Terms and WhatsApp Business Policy
  • API credentials and access tokens are encrypted at rest

Our use of Meta Platform data complies strictly with Meta's Platform Terms and is limited to the service the user has explicitly requested.

05 / Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent — for marketing and optional data collection
  • Contract — to fulfil agreed automation services
  • Legitimate Interests — analytics, security, service improvement
  • Legal Obligation — compliance with applicable law

06 / Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share only with trusted operational partners:

  • Meta / WhatsApp — for messaging API (Privacy Policy)
  • n8n GmbH — workflow infrastructure (Privacy Policy)
  • Make / Celonis — automation platform (Privacy Notice)
  • Cloud hosting providers — AWS, DigitalOcean (secure infrastructure only)

07 / Cookies

We use essential, analytics (anonymised), and functional cookies. You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings without affecting site functionality.

08 / Data Retention

Client project data: up to 3 years post-project. Contact form submissions: 12 months. WhatsApp message logs: max 90 days unless legally required.

09 / Your Rights

Under GDPR and India's DPDP Act you have the right to: access, correct, delete, or port your data; withdraw consent; and lodge a complaint with the relevant authority. Email us at privacy@sarvodayaautomation.com

10 / Children's Privacy

11 / International Transfers

Data may be processed outside India. We ensure appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent protections are in place.
